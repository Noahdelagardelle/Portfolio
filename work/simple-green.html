Simple Green

Simple Green All-Purpose Cleaner is safer non-toxic cleaner and degreaser that can be used on many surfaces. It’s a wonderful alternative to other house hold cleaners because of its environmentally friendly ingredients and effectiveness on stains. This is my take on what a more contemporary modern Simple Green could look like. My goal was to set it apart from the noisy design of alternative cleaners on the shelf today by giving it a simple and inviting modern look.
