About

Hi, my name is Noah. I am a designer and developer. I spend most of my time designing and thinking about the web. I am interested in creating beautiful things and experiences that are useful for human beings. I am passionate about responsive design, best practices, and web optimization.

I consider myself a full-process designer, which basically means I am interested in every part of the design process. I believe in staying curious, constantly learning, asking questions about the way things are done, and always looking for better ways to do things.

Currently studying graphic design at University of Wisconsin Stout. If you would like to get in touch feel free to send me an email and if you’re interested check out my résumé. Feel free to contact me about potential projects or just say hello!
